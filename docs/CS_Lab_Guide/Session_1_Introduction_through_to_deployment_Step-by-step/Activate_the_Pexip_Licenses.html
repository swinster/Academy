Activate the Pexip Licenses

Suggested completion time: 5 minutes

More information

Use section #8 of the lab sheet.

  1. On the Management Node go to Platform --> Licenses.
  2. Click Add License, enter the license key, and click Save.
    NOTE: be careful you do not enter any leading or trailing whitespace. Infinity will not complain, but the license may not activate.
  3. The features provided will appear on the license page when the license is activated. You can click on a feature to see its details, including the license key used.
