Introduction

Thanks for sticking with the labs so far. You are now into the second of the Level 2 labs, and as always, this lab continues where the last one left off. You will see a similar configuration to what you had previously. The external domain might have changed, but everything else should be the same.

During this lab, you will gain further insight into scaling an Infinity solution, understanding more advanced administrative tasks, applying techniques to tune your virtual environment, and how to customise the user experience.

In the topology diagram, we add third-party systems and show the call flow as devices connect through Infinity to other devices and systems.
